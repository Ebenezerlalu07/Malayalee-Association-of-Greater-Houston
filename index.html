Malayalee Association of Greater Houston, Story

Founded in 1987, Malayalee Association of Greater Houston (MAGH) has led numerous successful campaigns that have impacted Malayalee Communities in Stafford, Missouri city, Sugar Land and across Houston. Through events, telethons, sports tournaments, and community programs organized by MAGH, we reach out to all the Malayalee communities in Houston and surrounding areas. MAGH conducts various Celebrations throughout the year such as Republic Day, Independence Day and other Indian festivals. ONAM is considered the most popular and the biggest event among MAGH members. MAGH also conducts many Community Events such as Blood Drives, Passport Fairs, Vaccination Campaigns, as well as several Seminars that focused to help our youths and adults.
